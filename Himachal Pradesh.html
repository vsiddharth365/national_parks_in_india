Great Himalayan National Park


Location : Kullu
Established in : 1984
Area : 1171 km2
Flora : From the lofty pinesand spruces and the great, spreading horse chestnuts of the lower valleys, to the dense cushions and prostrate branches of the alpine herbs and junipers, the park presents an endless variety of vegetation.
Fauna : more than 375 faunal species. So far species of 31 mammals, 181 birds, 3 reptiles, 9 amphibians, 11 annelids, 17 mollusks and 127 insects. animals such as blue sheep, snow leopard, Himalayan brown bear, Himalayan tahr, musk deer, etc. are found.
Features : UNESCO World Heritage site

Inderkilla National Park


Location : Kullu district and 46.1 kilometers away from the Kullu Manali Airport
Established in : 2010
Area : 104 km2
Fauna : Rare mammals live here, such as brown and black bears, leopards and various mountain deer and goats. Over 250 species of birds have been recorded in the park.

Khirganga National Park


Location : Kullu, Himachal Pradesh
Established in : 2010
Area : 710 km2
Flora : Glossy green hills, dense green shrubberies, tall towering trees
Fauna : Several varieties of avifauna belonging to diverse species

Pin Valley National Park


Location : The Spiti Valley in the Lahaul and Spiti district, in the state of Himachal Pradesh
Established in : 1958
Area : 675 km2
Flora : The vegetation density is sparse, consisting mostly of alpine trees and groves of Himalayan cedar. Some plants within the park's alpine habitats have significant medicinal properties.
Fauna : Birds such as the Himalayan snowcock, chukar partridge, snow partridge, finches and choughs flourish in the park in summer.
Features : Part of Cold Desert Biosphere Reserve

Simbalbara National Park


Location : The Paonta Valley of Sirmour District, Himachal Pradesh, along its border with Haryana
Established in : 2010
Area : 27.88 km2
Fauna : Goral, Sambhar and Chittal are the common animals found here.
Features : There is a perennial stream in the valley. Simbalbara Forest Rest House is well-connected from Puruwala and provides a beautiful view of the valley.
